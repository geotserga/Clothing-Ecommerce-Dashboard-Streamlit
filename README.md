# Women's Clothing E-commerce Dashboard

This project is an interactive **Streamlit dashboard** that provides actionable insights into women's clothing e-commerce sales. The dataset is sourced from [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shilongzhuang/-women-clothing-ecommerce-sales-data/data), and the live dashboard is hosted on [Streamlit Cloud](https://clothing-ecommerce-dashboard-5plj5sm9xeg6kmshhijxna.streamlit.app/).

---

## **Project Steps**

1. **Define Key Metrics**  
   Identified the main business metrics, including total revenue, total orders, items sold, average order value, unique products, and average unit price.

2. **Perform EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis)**  
   Analyzed patterns in sales by color, size, SKU, and time (hourly, daily). This helped uncover trends and customer preferences.

3. **Build the Interactive Dashboard**  
   Developed a fully interactive dashboard with Streamlit, including:
   - Revenue trends over time
   - Top 25 SKUs by sales
   - Size distribution
   - Color popularity analysis
   - Hourly sales heatmap
   - Sales prediction for the next three months
   - Chat interface for querying key metrics

---

## **Dashboard Insights**

- **Top-selling sizes and colors:**  
  - XL size stands out as the highest-selling, providing guidance for inventory management and marketing strategies.  
  - Dark blue is the most popular color among customers, informing product design and stocking decisions.

- **Peak sales periods:**  
  - Sunday during 8:00, 14:00  
  - Monday during 10:00, 19:00  
  - Saturday during 11:00  
  These periods are ideal for scheduling promotions or targeted ads.

- **Sales forecast:**  
  - Sales are expected to fluctuate mostly between **$1,129 and $1,262**, reflecting the typical revenue range.  
  - Occasional dips to around **$886** and peaks up to **$1,423** indicate potential risks and opportunities.

- **Additional insights:**  
  - SKU-level analysis identifies the most popular products for focused marketing.  
  - Size and color performance charts help optimize inventory distribution.  
  - The dashboard supports filtering by date, size, and color, enabling dynamic analysis for different segments.
